**SRL Burwood** currently is still poorly served by it's tram and bus network. The express Route 201 will be fully replaced by SRL, saving students 15 minutes each way. However, Route 75 slowly visits limited destinations along Burwood Hwy and Route 70 falls short of the new station. Two other bus routes (767 and 903) serve the university and connect it to Box Hill. 

The Bus Map upgrades the 903 to an all-day 5 minute frequency, straightening it via Station St. Replacing the 767, a more useful and extended north-south route connecting Doncaster to Clarinda will run every 10 minutes. Additionally, a new route via Eley Rd connects students to residents east of the campus to Mitcham RS.

**SRL Box Hill:** Lowest improvement seen here out of the six stations. Box Hill is very well served by bus routes already, and as a terminus for SRL East it's waiting to be extended to Doncaster and beyond.
